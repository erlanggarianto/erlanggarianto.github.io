About me

Design, research, and engineering in one person

I'm Erlangga, a UX/Product Designer from Indonesia. I studied Information Systems at Universitas Indonesia, hold a Google UX Design certification, and have worked with B2B tech agencies, B2B collaboration platforms, B2B logistics SaaS, and B2C edtech across Indonesia, Malaysia, and UAE.

I work through the whole product process, from research and strategy to shipping with engineers. You won't catch me handing off Figma files and running!

I use AI across the whole process — research, planning, prototyping, testing — with tools like Claude and Copilot.
