About me

Design that connects dots others miss

I'm Erlangga, a UX/Product Designer from Indonesia with a Computer Science degree from Universitas Indonesia and a Google UX Design certification. I've worked across B2B tech agencies, logistics SaaS, and B2C edtech serving markets across Southeast Asia and EMEA.

I thrive at the intersection of design, research, data, and engineering. I work through the entire development process, from user interviews and product strategy to technical implementation and shipping alongside engineers. I don't hand off Figma files and disappear.

I actively use AI to improve workflows and build prototypes faster. Always learning. Always improving. Always sharing.
