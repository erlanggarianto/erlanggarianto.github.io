About me

Design, research, and engineering in one person

I'm Erlangga, a UX/Product Designer from Indonesia. I studied Computer Science at Universitas Indonesia and hold a Google UX Design certification. Over the years I've worked with B2B tech agencies, B2B logistics SaaS, and B2C edtech platforms across Southeast Asia and EMEA.

I work through the full product process, from user interviews and product strategy to collaborating with engineers until products ship. You won't catch me just handing off Figma files and running!

I also use AI to speed up my workflows and build prototypes faster. I'm always learning and always looking for a better way to do things.
